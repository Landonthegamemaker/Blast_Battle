HOW TO PLAY

PHASES: Each turn has 4 phases: Fast → Medium → Slow → Charged, then Movement.
CARDS: Select a card from your hand, then click "PLAY CARD" or "ATTACK". Defense cards can be played any phase.
RANGE: Melee=0 dist, Pistol/Shotgun/Grenade=1, AR/Missile=2, Sniper=3. Distance = spaces between you on the arena.
ARMOR: Armor reduces damage. Specialized armor is more effective. Durability drops with each hit.
AMMO: Each weapon has limited ammo. When depleted it's discarded automatically.
MOVEMENT: After all phases, move to an adjacent location. Some characters move 2 spaces.
LOCATIONS: Hero Zone buffs heroes, Villain Zone buffs villains, Armory lets you draw a weapon, Forge draws armor, Radiation deals 5 damage/turn, Scrap Heap discards a card.
WIN: Reduce the opponent's HP to 0.
